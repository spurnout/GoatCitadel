# GoatCitadel Vision

## Mission

Build a local-first AI operations platform that is trustworthy, proactive, and useful in real daily work without sacrificing operator control.

## Product Direction

GoatCitadel should evolve from a technical operator console into a human-friendly personal AI system with:

- strong default safety,
- transparent autonomy,
- workspace-level personalization,
- measurable quality and reliability.

## Guiding Principles

- Control before convenience.
- Clear explanations over hidden behavior.
- Additive compatibility over disruptive rewrites.
- Evidence-based self-improvement over intuition-only tuning.

## Near-Term Goals

- Raise prompt quality consistency with explicit gating.
- Harden orchestration around tool failures and constraints.
- Expand workspace personalization through guidance docs and scoped behavior.
- Improve non-technical usability across Mission Control pages.

## Mid-Term Goals

- Smarter proactive workflows with approval-aware autonomy budgets.
- Better multi-device operations and mesh visibility.
- Reliable long-term memory and replay-driven quality feedback loops.
- Mobile companion integration with constrained safe actions.

## Long-Term Goals

- Personal AI that can safely support life workflows, projects, and operations.
- Strong governance and auditability for every autonomous action.
- Extensible ecosystem of tools, skills, and integrations without safety erosion.
